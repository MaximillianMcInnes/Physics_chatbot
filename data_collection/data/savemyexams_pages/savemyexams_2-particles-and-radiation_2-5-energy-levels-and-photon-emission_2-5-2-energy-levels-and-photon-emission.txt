Line Spectra & Energy Levels Energy levels can be represented as a series of horizontal lines The line at the bottom with the greatest negative energy represents the ground state The lines above the ground state with decreasing energies represent excited states The line at the top, usually 0 V or infinity , represents the ionisation energy Energy Levels in a Hydrogen Atom A photon is emitted when an electron moves from a higher energy state to a lower energy state. The energy of the emitted photon is equal to the difference in energy between the energy levels in the transition.
