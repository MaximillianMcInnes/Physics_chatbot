A cyclist travels a distance of 20 m at a constant speed then decelerates to a traffic light 5 m ahead. The whole journey takes 3.5 s. Calculate the average speed of the cyclist.
Step 1: Write the average speed equation
Average speed = total distance ÷ total time
Step 2: Calculate the total distance
Total distance = 20 + 5 = 25 m
Step 3: Calculate the average speed
Average speed = 25 ÷ 3.5 = 7.1 m s -1
