Here is a maths tip to rejig your memory on reciprocals: The reciprocal of a value is 1 / value For example, the reciprocal of a whole number such as 2 equals ½ Conversely, the reciprocal of ½ is 2 If the number is already a fraction, the numerator and denominator are ‘flipped’ round The reciprocal of a number is 1 ÷ number In the case of the resistance R , this becomes 1/ R To get the value of R from 1/ R , you must calculate 1 ÷ your answer You can also use the reciprocal button on your calculator (labelled either x -1 or 1/x, depending on your calculator)
